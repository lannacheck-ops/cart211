Final Investigation Report

1. Euphemisms Exposed
OmniCore used soft terms like “enhancing your experience,” “operational metrics,” and “trusted partners.” Behind these friendly phrases are hidden practices of surveillance, data profiling, and profit-driven data exchange.

2. Unlimited Data Exhaust
Every scroll, tap, pause, and movement produces data exhaust. Companies collect it endlessly — often without clear limits or awareness.

3. Ads Built From Your Data
Ads appear “personalized” but are really predictions about your vulnerabilities. This system uses your data exhaust to shape what you see, guiding your choices under the illusion of convenience.

4. The Myth of Trusted Partners
“Trusted partners” are simply businesses exchanging user data for profit. Your information is passed between companies, forming invisible networks you never knowingly consented to.

5. Surveillance Capitalism Is Everywhere
These practices hide in daily life — social media, shopping, navigation. Awareness is the only defense against subtle manipulation and targeted persuasion.
